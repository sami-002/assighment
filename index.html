Afghanistan has a rich oral tradition, and proverbs (ضرب‌المثل) are commonly used in conversation to share wisdom. These sayings often convey life lessons about patience, perseverance, family, and community. This site highlights a few beloved Afghan proverbs in Dari along with English translations, reflecting the cultural values of the Afghan people.

صبر تلخ است ولیکن بر شیرین دارد

Patience is bitter, but its fruit is sweet.

دوستی در زمان خوشی و بدی است

Friendship is in times of joy and sorrow.
