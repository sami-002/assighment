صد دایه مهربان به مادر نمی‌رسد

One hundred kind nurses cannot equal one mother.

This proverb emphasizes that a mother's love and care are unmatched. Even many other caregivers cannot replace a mother's influence and affection. In Afghan culture, it highlights the invaluable role of a mother in raising a child and shows the deep respect held for mothers.
